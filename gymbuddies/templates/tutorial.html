Tutorial

Please read through this quick tutorial!

Viewing your Dashboard

After you successfully create your profile, you will land on this dashboard page, where you can view a schedule containing your current matches and a short summary of your profile.

How to Find a Buddy

The "Find a Buddy" tab shows you a list of potential gym partners, who you can decide to pass or match with. When matching, make sure that you select a time out of the gray boxes, which are their available times. You can view your outgoing requests in the "Outgoing Requests" tab.

Managing Incoming Requests

In the "Incoming Requests" tab, you will be shown a list of your incoming requests. To view details about the requester and the see the times they suggested, click "View/Modify Request".

From this popup, you can either accept the times that they suggested, or suggest new times. Modify times by cliking on the gray boxes, and send the request back to the person. Note that they can also modify your request and send it back as well!

Managing your Matches

From the "Matches" tab, you can view a list of your current and past matches. To unmatch with someone, simply click the "unmatch" button.

Editing your Profile

You can edit your profile and availability in the "Profile" tab. TIP: Our algorithm matches people based on the fields in profiles. If you are seeing no one in "Find a Buddy", edit your profile to see more potential buddies.

Settings

In the "Settings" tab, you can change your notification settings, block users, or delete your account.
